## Keyboard shortcuts

- `↑, ←, Pg Up, k` Go to previous slide
- `↓, →, Pg Dn, Space, j` Go to next slide
- `Home` Go to first slide
- `End` Go to last slide
- `Number` + Return Go to specific slide
- `b` Toggle blackout
- `m` Toggle mirrored
- `c` Clone slideshow
- `p` Toggle presenter mode
- `?, h` Toggle this help
