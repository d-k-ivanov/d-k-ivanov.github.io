# English Names for Large Numbers

Almost entirely taken from [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Names_of_large_numbers#Extensions_of_the_standard_dictionary_numbers).

- The **Short Scale** is used in the United States and most English-speaking countries today.
- The **traditional English** names were used in the UK and other countries before the adoption of the short scale.
- The **Long Scale** is used in EU countries and some other parts of the world.
